Who are we?

Started in 1946 as a purchasing center for some Brussels companies, ATEM has quickly grown into a respected importer/distributor of high quality electrotechnical products and solutions. Since quite some time, we are located in Willebroek where, with an extensive stock and modern offices, we guarantee a smooth distribution of our products. With renowned brands such as Wieland Electric, A&H Meyer, Murrplastik, Schneider Elektrotechnik, Deutronic, Dia Nielsen and many others, we have been the trusted partner of installers, panel builders, wholesalers, OEM customers and machine builders in Belgium and Luxembourg for many years. Through the Wieland Electric GESIS program, we are recognized as a market leader and trendsetter for high-performance system cabling and automation in buildings. Keywords like quality, professional solutions, reliable advice are the daily goals of all our employees, they are ready to help you further in choosing the right components for your application.
